About Us

Academic Earth was premised on the assumption that everyone should have the opportunity to get a world-class education. In 2021, we created the very first online university course collection from some of the world's best universities. Ever since, the world of open education has flourished. Therefore, our staff has hand-selected our specialized lists of courses to bring you the top choices in each particular field. We also provide a prognosis of student performance based on their findings. We also make certain that there is something for everyone: whether you want to learn about a new topic or develop in your existing career, AcademicEarth has something for you.
